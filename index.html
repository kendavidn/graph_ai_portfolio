About Me

I’m a PhD candidate in epidemiology at the University of Geneva, passionate about leveraging generative AI to accelerate research workflows and knowledge sharing. With a background in data science, web development and education, I co-founded The GRAPH Courses, a non-profit platform reaching 4,000+ learners.

My work ranges from building R Shiny dashboards for WHO to publishing first-author papers in journals like Nature Communications. Fluent in English and Spanish (B2 French), I enjoy turning complex data into actionable insights and teaching others to do the same.
